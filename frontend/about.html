About HERathon

Our Mission

HERathon's mission is to bridge the gender gap in STEM fields by providing a supportive, interactive, and gamified platform that empowers young women to learn, compete, and excel in STEM fields.

Our Vision

We envision a STEM industry where women are equally represented as leaders, innovators, and creators. By fostering early passion and providing continuous mentorship, HERathon aims to be the catalyst for the next generation of female STEM pioneers.

The Team

Our team is composed of passionate developers, designers, and educators dedicated to making STEM accessible for everyone. We operate driven by the genuine belief that opportunities should be for all.

Partners

We collaborate with industry-leading companies, non-profit organizations, and educational institutions who share our commitment to diversity and inclusion in the tech space.
