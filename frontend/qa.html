Help & FAQs

Common Technical Issues

  • I can't log in or reset password: Ensure you are using the correct email associated with your account. If you forgot your password, the reset link might be in your spam folder.
  • Dashboard not loading: Try clearing your browser cache or switching to a different network. Make sure you are on a supported modern browser.
  • Can't submit challenge solution: Ensure your file sizes are within the 10MB limit and in the accepted formats (link, PDF, DOCX, ZIP).
